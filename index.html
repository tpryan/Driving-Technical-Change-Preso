The Problem

Skeptics

The People in your neighborhood

These are caracatures.

People are much more complex.

This is a way of understanding them

Uninformed

  • Symptoms
    • Don't know
  • Cause
    • Never came across it
  • Prognosis
    • Easy to change
    • Initial change likely to be other persona

Herd

  • Symptoms
    • “No one told us to use the technique.”
    • “Are we allowed to do that?.”
  • Cause
    • Look to a strong leader to tell them what to do
  • Prognosis
    • Easiest to change
    • You have to be willing to lead

Cynic

  • Symptoms
    • Have not tried technique
    • Question effectiveness
    • Question degree of effectiveness
  • Cause
    • Doubt without proof
    • Trying to score points
  • Prognosis
    • They can be converted with miles and miles of proof

Burned

  • Symptoms
    • Had past failures with technique.
  • Cause
    • Umm… Past failures
  • Prognosis
    • Hard to change
    • Can be done if you prove previous implementation was:
      • misapplied
      • flawed
      • deficient

Time Crunched

  • Symptoms
    • ““I don’t have time for it.”
  • Cause
    • Do not believe they can afford the time to learn and implement
  • Prognosis
    • They can be converted if you prove that technique will save them time

Boss

  • Symptoms
    • Don’t care about this level of detail
  • Cause
    • Not really their bailiwick
  • Prognosis
    • Hit or miss
    • Make your solution a fix to their problems

Irrational

  • Symptoms
    • Resist any attempts to introduce method
    • Hides as another type
    • Brings up objections that don’t conform with previous behavior
  • Cause
    • Doesn’t matter
  • Prognosis
    • Can’t change, don’t worry

Tactics

What to focus on

Two Types

Universal

  • Expertise
  • Delivery
  • Demonstration
  • Trust

Situational

  • Compromise
  • Synergy
  • Pressure
  • Bridging
  • Publicity

Expertise

  • Use these techniques for yourself
  • Don’t wait for your organization
  • Gain an expertise not just a familiarity

Expertise - personas

Effective on:

  • Uninformed
  • Herd
  • Cynic
  • Burned

Delivery

  • Be passionate
  • Don’t be zealous
  • “Have you tried…” vs “You should…”

Delivery - personas

Effective on:

  • Uninformed
  • Cynic
  • Burned
  • Boss
  • Irrational

Demonstrate

  • Show, don’t tell
  • Produce a solution in a weekend
  • Solve a group problem

Demonstrate - personas

Effective on:

  • Uninformed
  • Herd
  • Time Crunched
  • Cynic
  • Burned
  • Boss
  • Irrational

Compromise

  • Remove restrictions in return for using technique
  • Especially if technique obviates restriction

Compromise - personas

Effective on:

  • Uninformed
  • Cynic
  • Burned
  • Boss
  • Irrational

Synergy

  • Connect implementing your technique with a larger concern
    • Security
    • Regulations Compliance

Synergy - personas

Effective on:

  • Boss

Pressure

  • Network Externalities
    • Electronic peer pressure
    • Create a solution that people really need that relies on your technique

Pressure - personas

Effective on:

  • Uninformed
  • Herd
  • Time Crunched
  • Cynic
  • Burned

Bridging

Create something enticing that is halfway between where you are and where you want to be

Bridging - personas

Effective on:

  • Herd
  • Time Crunched

Publicity

  • Get your code reviewed
  • Open Source your solution
  • Apply for awards

Publicity - personas

Effective on:

  • Uninformed
  • Cynic
  • Burned
  • Boss

Trust

  • Be honest
  • Don't lie by:
    • omission
    • spin
  • Avoid FUD

Trust - personas

Effective on:

  • Uninformed
  • Herd
  • Time Crunched
  • Cynic
  • Burned
  • Boss
  • Irrational
